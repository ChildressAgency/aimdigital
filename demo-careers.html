Brand Manager

AIM Digital Media is now hiring Brand Managers. We are a successful mobile digital marketing firm based in Fredericksburg, Virginia. We seel smart, ambitious, and hardworking Independent Sales & Marketing Representatives to join our team.

This is an independent position where you will generate new client accounts and provide marketing consulting to new and existing clients by selling mobile digital advertising.

The successful candidate will:

  • Be able to work in a fast paced, professional environment
  • Manage multiple projects at a time
  • Demonstrate initiative
  • Have excellent organizational skills
  • Have internet and computer skills and be willing to learn
  • Have an outgoing personality
  • Have great communication skills
  • Possess a valid Virginia driver's license
  • AIM Digital Media is an Equal Opportunity Employer
